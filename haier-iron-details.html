Overview

Built for smooth everyday ironing

The Haier HSR-8063 combines steam and dry ironing support for regular household use. It gives users flexible fabric handling with adjustable temperature and steam levels, while the ceramic soleplate helps protect clothes and glide more smoothly across the surface.
